The evening

Cocktails, sushi,
and a green rooftop

An invite-only night built for tasting — not a festival, not a club takeover.

I

Where

Garden rooftop overlooking the High Line

Soft light through the leaves, the city humming below. The setting stays intimate on purpose — a place to linger over a glass and a piece of nigiri, not rush a circuit of booths.

II

Who’s behind the bar

Talented up-and-coming bartenders

Original cocktails built for the room — bright, food-friendly, and centered on products worth discovering. Signature serves and tasting pours, depending on the brand.

III

What’s served

Primarily nigiri & hand rolls

Chef-prepared sushi moving through the evening so every sip has something clean and savory beside it. No banquet buffet — just a steady rhythm of plates and pours.
